About Me

about-me

Nice to meet you! I'm Arnold, an Animator & Motion Designer with 4+ years' experience crafting visuals that move, engage, and inspire.

I blend creativity with technical precision, using After Effects, Premiere Pro, Blender, Cinema 4D, and AI-powered workflows to bring ideas to life.

I've collaborated with brands like Visa, Safaricom (M-Pesa), LG, Duracoat Paints, and Bamburi Cement, delivering visuals that connect and stand out.

For me, design isn't just work — it's a space to experiment, innovate, and give back through open collaboration.
